Catfish Curry

Description

Looking for an impressive, satisfying dinner that comes together in under an hour? Your search ends with this surprisingly simple catfish curry recipe.

"Curry" refers to a dish that features a seasoned sauce. Curries are often associated with South Asian cuisine, but many cultures have their own versions of the dish. This catfish curry, for instance, has West African roots.

Ingredients

  • Catfish
  • Onions
  • Seasonings
  • Flour and Butter
  • Fresh Herbs
  • Rice

Steps

  1. Cook the catfish
  2. Make the curry
  3. Serve the catfish curry
